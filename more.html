Meet the Players

Popsicle Pav

Tiger ice cream matches perfectly with Popsicle Pav's hair. Maybe that's why she loves it so much. And who doesn't love tigers?

Mila a la Mode

Mila a la Mode loves to go crazy with the fun flavours! Bubblegum and strawberry? Yes please! Green tea ice cream? Yuk! Who would want grass flavoured ice cream?? A cup is a good idea, so it doesn't get too messy.

Malty Mark

Malty Mark is a true ice cream sommelier. Only the finest ingredients for Mark: fresh mango sorbet and rich dark chocolate artisan gelato topped with hand picked organic shade-grown walnuts. Waffle cones are the only suitable cones to accompany such a delacacy.

Affogato Andrea

Don’t even think about bringing chocolate anywhere near Affogato Andrea! Chocolate cones, ice cream, syrup, BIG nono! Andrea only trusts a smooth vanilla or a peaceful green tea melt—safely tucked in a cup. And if you’re feeling fancy, a cherry on top is the only acceptable bonus.

Parfait Pippa

Parfait Pippa is scoop-er picky when it comes to ice cream. She insists on the perfect swirl of mango and green tea topped with some chocolate syrup. Only the finest of waffle cones for Pippa, no plain cone nonsense.

About the Game

West Van Soft is a card game where players compete to create the drippiest ice cream combinations. Mix and match flavors, toppings, and cones to score points and become the ruler of West Van!

Perfect for competitive players. Suitable for 3-8 players, ages Mila and up.
